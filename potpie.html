Chicken Pot Pie



Ingredients

  • 1 Can diced potatoes 15oz
  • 1 bag 12oz frozen peas/carrots mix thawed
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen mushrooms chopped
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 3 chicken breasts cooked. Diced.(should yield about 3-4 cups chopped chicken)
  • 2-3 cups chicken broth (start with 2, you can always add more
  • 1/2 cup milk (or almond milk)
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup 10oz (cream of potato or cream of celery work too)
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 tsp dried thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 2 pie crusts
  • 1 deep dish round caserole pan or glassware


Cooking Directions

  1. Pre bake bottom crust in a dish, follow box directions. Typically 425o for 10-12min - This keeps the bottom crust crisp instead of doughy
  2. Combine canned/thawed vagtables and set aside
  3. Cook chicken, let cool, cut into cubes with kitchen shears, set aside
  4. Melt butter, add onion and cook over medium high heat. Stir in flour and seasonings. Gradually stir in milk, broth, and bring to a boil, stirring constantly until thickened. At this point you can add 1 can of cream of chicken soup instead of heavy cream to add flavor and creaminess.
  5. Add chicken and remaining combined vegtables to creamy onion mixture
  6. Pour into pre-baked pie crust
  7. Top with additional pie crust. Cut slits in top.
  8. Bake 35-40min until lightly browned or bubbling. Note: contents will most likelybubble over, so place on a cookie sheet rpior to baking
  9. Let sit for 15minutes before serving
