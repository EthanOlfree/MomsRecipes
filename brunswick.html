Easy Crockpot Brunswick Stew



Ingredients

  • One Large Onion (diced)
  • Two 10oz cans of beef Castleberry barbeque beef in barbeque sauce
  • Two 10oz cans of shredded chicken
  • Two 10oz cansof pork Castleberry barbeque pork in barbeque sauce
  • One 150z can of cream corn style corn
  • One 15oz can of whole kernel corn
  • One 28oz can of whole tomatoes
  • One 28oz can of crushed tomatoes
  • One 18oz bottle barbeque sauce
  • One tablespoon Frank's Red Hot sauce
  • Two cups of water
  • One 15oz can of potatoes diced
  • Two cans Lima beans


Directions

  1. Add onions, chicken, beef, pork, corn, barbeque sauce, and hot sauce to the pot
  2. Break up whole tomatoes into chunks and add to mixture without straining
  3. Add crushed tomatoes to mixture
  4. Add Two cups of water and stir
  5. Place lid and set cooker on low cook for 4 to 5 hours
  6. Add diced potatoes and cook 1 more hour
  7. Serve with buttermilk cornbread or garlic bread

Note:

If brands are not available use local brands
