Crockpot Mac and Cheese



Ingredients

  • 6 Cups of dry macaroni noodle
  • Butter
  • 4 Cups(or 1cup cans) of evaporated milk
  • 6 Cups of shredded cheese
  • salt and pepper
  • frozen pre-chopped onions (optional)
  • Optional - Crockpot cooking bag to help with cleaning and storage


Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook the macaroni noodles and put them in the crock pot with the butter
  2. Add the evaporated milk
  3. Add the shredded cheese, salt, pepper, and chopped onions(optional)
  4. Mix the ingredients well in the pot
  5. On LOW SETTING cook for 3hours or less. Do Not stir while it is cooking

Note: makes a large amount, halving recipe is recommended for smaller meals.
